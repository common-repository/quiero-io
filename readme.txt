== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= What do you actually do? =

Quiero helps you understand the way your website visitors engage with your website by key metrics – scroll depth, time on page & pages viewed for every visitor that enters your site.

= What is the Combined Engagement Score? =

The Combined Engagement Score (or CES), is a combination of several key behavioral metrics to a single score.

= How do I use these in my campaigns? =

Quiero works with the Facebook and Google Pixels, firing ‘custom events’ directly into them.

= What's the difference between the Basic and the Pro tier? =

The Basic tier features a free WP-plugin app that will deliver Scroll Depth, Page Depth & Time on Page directly into your ad platform.

= How do I install Quiero? =

It’s super easy. Just add our WP-Plugin to your store. That’s it. No further setup required.

= Do you take any of my data? =

Nope. We don’t have any access to any of your visitor or user data.

= Are you GDPR compliant? =

Yes. Absolutely.
